We Are Stronger Together

The ASK ESP Union represents approximately 2,500 educators within Salem-Keizer Public Schools.

Promoting excellence in education and student achievement.

Advocating for the rights of education support professionals.

Learn More

What Drives Us

Members

Create and maintain job security, increase wages, improve safety, collaboration and work conditions.

Students

Classrooms that are conducive to learning, access to school counselors, nurses, and educators.

Community

Access to opportunities, schools that bring together academics, health, and community engagement.

Who We Are

The ASK ESP Union represents professionals in various areas, promoting excellence in education and student achievement.

Administrative and Clerical

Managing office operations, secretarial tasks, and clerical support.

Facilities and Maintenance

Ensuring clean and well-maintained facilities, including HVAC, utilities, and construction.

Community and Student Services

Supporting students and community through outreach and specialized programs.

Transportation

Providing safe and efficient transportation for students and staff.

Technology and Support

Managing IT infrastructure, software development, and technical support.

Child Care and Education Support

Assisting in child care, instructional support, and educational programs.
