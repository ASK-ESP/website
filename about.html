Who We Are

The Association of Salem Keizer Education Support Professionals (ASK ESP) is an organization committed to advocating for education support professionals within the Salem-Keizer Public Schools. We represent approximately 2,500 educators and are the largest union of classified support staff in the state of Oregon. Our mission is to promote excellence in education and to advocate for student achievement.

What We Believe In

We believe in fostering an inclusive environment that values diversity, professional development, and the well-being of every student and staff member.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote excellence in education, advocate for student achievement, and support the well-being of our members.
