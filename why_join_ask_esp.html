The ASK ESP Union represents approximately 2,500 educators within Salem-Keizer Public Schools.

Promoting excellence in education and student achievement.

Advocating for the rights of education support professionals.

Employee Assistance Program

As part of our union-negotiated health benefits package, we offer a comprehensive Employee Assistance Program. Services range from crisis help lines to counseling and legal services.

Questions about Benefits

For detailed questions about your health insurance, sick leave, leaves of absence, or other benefits, you can contact SKSD at 503-399-5556.

About Dues

Dues are on a sliding scale, depending on your FTE and salary. They are collected 9 months of the year through payroll deduction and go towards programs and services that support our members.

  • Annual Salary FTE (percentage of 40 hrs) Dues (Oct-Jun)
  • $65,259 or more 51% - 100% $66.39
  • $32,630 - $65,258 51% - 100% $49.39
  • $16,315 - $32,629 51% - 100% $43.89
  • $16,315 - $32,629 50% or less $33.89
  • $8,157 - $16,314 50% or less $23.89
